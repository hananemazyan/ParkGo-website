About Us

Our Journey in Creating a Smart Parking Solution

Our smart parking project was carried out by female students pursuing a bachelor's degree in systems information and digital transformation. We leveraged our expertise and passion for innovation to create a modern and efficient parking solution. Our goal is to provide a quality service that simplifies the lives of our users while contributing to smarter parking space management. We take pride in our work and are committed to continuously improving and developing our solution to meet the changing needs of our customers.
